LinkedIn Credentials Required

To post to LinkedIn, you need to configure your credentials:

  1. Go to LinkedIn Developer Portal
  2. Create an app and get OAuth 2.0 credentials
  3. Get your access token and person URN
  4. Update your .env file or use the configuration page

How It Works
  1. Enter your LinkedIn API credentials in the .env file or configuration page
  2. Click "Test Connection" to verify your credentials
  3. Write your post content (up to 3000 characters)
  4. Click "Post to LinkedIn" to publish directly to your LinkedIn profile
  5. Your post will appear on LinkedIn immediately
