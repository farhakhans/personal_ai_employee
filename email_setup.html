Setup Instructions
Step 1: Get Gmail App Password
  1. Go to Google Account Settings
  2. Click on Security
  3. Enable 2-Step Verification (if not already enabled)
  4. Go to App Passwords
  5. Select "Mail" and your device
  6. Click "Generate" and copy the 16-character password
Step 2: Enter Credentials
Not your regular Gmail password - use the App Password from Step 1
Step 3: Test Connection

After saving settings, click the button below to test your Gmail connection:

Test Email Preview

Emails sent from the dashboard will be delivered to your real Gmail inbox!
